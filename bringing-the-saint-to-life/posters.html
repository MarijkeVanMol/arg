bringing-saint-luke-to-life

The posters all have three words: ‘DID YOU KNOW?’; followed by facts about Saint Luke. On the bottom of the posters there are QR-codes, links, numbers… that all hold more information. This way the player is one step closer to solving the mystery around Saint Luke and his relation to Sint Lucas Antwerpen. Cato Speltincx and Lucas Struijck worked on the design and Ezra Babski did the text of the poster.
