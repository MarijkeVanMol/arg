bringing-the-saint-to-life

At the start of the academic year, we set on an ambitious project to create an ARG — an alternate reality game — that would use all our existing skills in digital media to create a unique experience.

Created by students in the Digital Master Programme of Sint Lucas Antwerpen:
Ezra Babski, Odis Babski, Anna Beirinckx, Magalie Fierens, Iren Loontjens, Michelle Maes, Cato Speltincx, Fien Stappaerts, Lucas Struijck & Marijke Van Mol
